What are we?

Entropy is a fundamental concept that everything in the universe must inevitably obey. It always increases in any natural process.

Except in anomalies like LIFE , understood fully only by the best of Designers, Allah Almighty.

The increasing disorder or randomness in the universe is part of Allah's divine trial, He planned it that way so there will be a time when WE MUST DIE, life must end, and the whole Universe must seize to have any energy, heat in it.

In the end, it's just the burned-out cores of lifeless stars for the universe and cold, dark graves for us, to sleep until we awaken for the judgment call.

But we've been given precious time to live, to create memories, and to be good to others and do good, submitting ourselves to HIM.
InshaAllah.

You've beautifully articulated a perspective that intertwines scientific principles with spiritual beliefs. The concept of entropy, the measure of disorder in the universe, is indeed a profound aspect of thermodynamics and cosmology. In the context of your beliefs, it highlights a divine design and purpose behind the natural processes of the universe.This perspective can be a source of deep reflection and inspiration, emphasizing the temporary nature of our existence and the importance of living a meaningful, virtuous life. Embracing both the scientific understanding and spiritual insights can offer a holistic view of our place in the cosmos and our responsibilities within it. InshaAllah, may we all strive to make the most of our time and actions in this world.
